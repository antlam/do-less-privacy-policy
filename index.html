Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 7, 2025

Do Less does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All data entered in the app — including your daily goals — is stored entirely on your device and is not shared with any external servers or third parties. The app does not use analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies of any kind.

No login is required and no account is created or managed. The app does not request access to sensitive system permissions such as location, contacts, microphone, camera, or health data.

This privacy-first approach is intentional.
